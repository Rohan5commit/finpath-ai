# FinPath AI Elevator Pitch

FinPath AI helps students and young adults understand their money in plain English.
Instead of giving generic budgeting tips, it turns income, expenses, savings goals, and habits into a personalized financial health snapshot, a savings roadmap, explainers, and an action checklist. The product is practical, educational, and scalable — it can start as a consumer web app and grow into a financial wellness platform for schools, employers, and fintech partners.
