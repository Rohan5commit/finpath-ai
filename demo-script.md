# FinPath AI Demo Script

## Target length

2.5 to 3 minutes

## Opening (0:00 - 0:20)

“FinPath AI is an AI-powered financial planning and literacy platform built for students and young adults. Instead of generic advice, it turns someone’s actual income, expenses, habits, and goals into a simple plan they can act on immediately.”

## Step 1 — Land on homepage (0:20 - 0:40)

- Show clean landing page
- Point out the core value proposition
- Mention that judges can use seeded demos instantly without signing up

Talk track:
“We designed this to be fast for users and fast for judges. You can jump directly into a realistic demo profile with one click.”

## Step 2 — Open seeded demo account (0:40 - 1:10)

- Launch Maya or Jordan demo from the homepage
- Arrive on dashboard
- Highlight score, monthly cash flow, and top recommendations

Talk track:
“Here, FinPath gives a personalized financial health snapshot. It doesn’t just say ‘save more’ — it shows what’s working, what’s risky, and what to do next.”

## Step 3 — Show budget + goals (1:10 - 1:40)

- Scroll to budget breakdown
- Show goal roadmap and whether the user is on track
- Call out milestone pacing and automation suggestion

Talk track:
“The budget view translates raw spending into clear categories, while the goal planner shows whether each savings target is realistic on the current path.”

## Step 4 — Show spending insights + explainers (1:40 - 2:00)

- Show risk alerts
- Show explainer cards

Talk track:
“This is where the financial literacy layer matters. FinPath doesn’t just flag issues — it explains them in plain English so users actually learn why a change matters.”

## Step 5 — Scenario simulator (2:00 - 2:20)

- Increase savings by 20%
- Trim flexible spending a few percent
- Show how the first goal arrives sooner

Talk track:
“The scenario simulator helps users understand tradeoffs before they make them. That makes the product useful for decision-making, not just tracking.”

## Step 6 — AI follow-up question (2:20 - 2:45)

- Ask: “What should I cut first if I still want to go out with friends?”
- Show grounded response

Talk track:
“The AI assistant stays simple, practical, and non-jargony. It helps users make sense of their plan without crossing into regulated advice.”

## Close (2:45 - 3:00)

“FinPath AI is a scalable fintech + education product: useful today for individuals, and expandable tomorrow for schools, employers, and fintech partners. It solves a real pain point with a product that judges can understand immediately.”
