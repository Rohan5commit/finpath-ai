# FinPath AI Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1 — Title

- FinPath AI
- AI-powered financial planning and financial literacy for students and young adults
- One-line promise: turn money stress into a clear, personalized action plan

## Slide 2 — Problem

- Students and young adults face real money stress with little personalized guidance
- Financial education is often generic, intimidating, or disconnected from daily decisions
- Budgeting tools track numbers but rarely teach better habits or give understandable next steps

## Slide 3 — Solution

- FinPath AI converts a simple financial profile into:
  - financial health snapshot
  - budget breakdown
  - savings roadmap
  - risk alerts
  - explainers
  - action checklist
  - scenario planning

## Slide 4 — Product Demo Flow

- Start with seeded judge demo account
- Open dashboard instantly
- Show health score + summary
- Run scenario simulator
- Ask follow-up question in AI assistant
- End on clear action checklist

## Slide 5 — What makes it innovative

- Combines deterministic financial planning logic with structured AI explanation
- Personalization is practical, not gimmicky
- Teaches financial literacy while helping users act immediately
- Demo-safe fallback keeps the experience usable even without live AI

## Slide 6 — Technical execution

- Next.js 16 + TypeScript + Tailwind
- Server routes for AI analysis, chat, and scenario planning
- NVIDIA NIM integration for structured outputs
- Seeded demo accounts, CI, and Vercel deployment workflow

## Slide 7 — Why now / why this matters

- Young adults are managing inflation, debt, rent pressure, and unstable entry-level income
- Financial confidence directly affects academic performance, stress, and early-career decisions
- Simple, explainable AI can dramatically lower the barrier to financial planning

## Slide 8 — Market and scalability

- Consumer launch for students + early-career users
- University partnerships for financial wellness programs
- Fintech partnerships for embedded education + goal nudges
- Employer programs for internship and new-hire financial onboarding

## Slide 9 — Responsible AI + trust

- Educational, non-regulated framing
- No guaranteed outcomes or risky recommendations
- Deterministic financial math stays as the source of truth
- AI improves usability, clarity, and engagement

## Slide 10 — Closing ask

- FinPath AI is a practical, scalable AI fintech product with immediate social impact
- Strong fit for Nexforge judging criteria: innovation, execution, relevance, and feasibility
